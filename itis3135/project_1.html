Project 1

Project Overview

  • My application is a website for my clients car detailing business where customers can look at prices and browse through information about the company.
  • The intended user is anyone who is interested in purchasing and scheduling a car detailing service.
  • The website will have a home page, about page, services page, contact page, and recent work page.

Wireframe and Site Map:

Page Design:

The pages that I will be implementing are home, about, services, contact, and recent work.

Home

  • Purpose: Attract customers by providing pictures, feedback, and reviews.
  • Audience: Customers
  • Content: Logo, pictures, reviews.
  • Interactivity: No.
  • Asking User to enter data: No
  • Contain buttons, hyperlinks, or dropdowns: No
  • Special Notes: Make it visually appealing.

About

  • Purpose: Display information about the owner.
  • Audience: Customers.
  • Content: Text.
  • Asking User to enter data: No
  • Contain buttons, hyperlinks, or dropdowns: No
  • Interactivity: None.
  • Special Notes: Make it visually appealing.

Services

  • Purpose: Provide detailed information about a specific service.
  • Audience: Customers.
  • Content: Service images, detailed description, price.
  • Asking User to enter data: No
  • Contain buttons, hyperlinks, or dropdowns: Yes
  • Interactivity: Dropdown menue to display different services.
  • Special Notes: Display differnt types of services.

Contact

  • Purpose: Display contact info to customers.
  • Audience: Customers.
  • Content: Contact info and submitting customer infro.
  • Asking User to enter data: Yes
  • Contain buttons, hyperlinks, or dropdowns: Yes
  • Interactivity: This page displays contact infro and doesn't have any interactivity
  • Special Notes: Contact info will contain email, phone number, address and ask customers to enter phone number and email.

Recent Work

  • Purpose: Show new customers examples of past detailing services.
  • Audience: Customers.
  • Content: Slideshow of pictures.
  • Asking User to enter data: No
  • Data Validation: Yes
  • Contain buttons, hyperlinks, or dropdowns: Yes
  • Interactivity: Move through a slideshow of pcitures.
  • Special Notes: Slideshow will have a button to scroll across pictures.

Dynamic Functionality

  • Navigation Bar and Header/Footer: Throughout all pages
  • Form Validation: Ensure that user contact infro is correct and is not missing.
  • Image Slideshow: Slide of images from previous customers.
  • Form and Dropdown: Use dropdown to select and diplay price and infor about different services and a form to input customer info
